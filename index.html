Dylan Cox

Technology & Applied Studies Teacher

This site has been designed to showacse my teacher identity, experience and professional development

My passion for teaching centres around a keen interest in Design and Technology, and the ambition to inspire a similar interest in my students. I gained experience and specialisation in Industrial Technology through my years of industry experience in woodmachinery, joinery and construction, before furthering my studies at the Australian Catholic University. My teaching practice places emphasis on the changing nature of design, technologies and applied studies, while continuing to pass on traditional time-tested design and manufacturing techniques and processes.
