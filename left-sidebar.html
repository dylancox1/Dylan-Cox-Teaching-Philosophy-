About Me

Technology & Applied Studies Teacher

Passionate about all things design and technology, with a particular interest in industrial technology and woodworking.

Experience

I began to develop my interests and gained timber industry experience in the business I grew up in, spending a lot of my time around lifetime tradesman, learning the basics of woodworking, industrial technology and design. I spent many years working as a labourer throughout high school, engaging in joinery, woodmachinery and cabinet making. I continued to work in the industry after completing my schooling, broadening my skills and gaining experience in carpentry, construction and various material technologies as a result.

In 2021 I decided to continue my studies at the Australian Catholic University, undertaking a Bachelor of Education Secondary / Bachelor of Arts Design and Technology. I have since developed my abilities and skills not only in various Technologies & Applied Studies, but developed my teacher identity and passion for instilling similar interests in my students. To this day I continue to engage with the timber industry, working and manufacturing to various extents as I develop my own skills, sharing it with my students.
